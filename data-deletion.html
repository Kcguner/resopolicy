Data Deletion Request

Reso Application

You can request the deletion of your personal data stored in the Reso application. Below you will find instructions on how to initiate this process.

Data That Will Be Deleted

When your data deletion request is processed, the following information will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account information (email, name)
  • Your profile information
  • Venues you added and your votes
  • Your in-app preferences
  • Social media connection information

How to Delete Your Data

1Request via Email

Send an email including the following information:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Name associated with your account

2From Within the App

Through the Reso application:

  1. Go to the Profile tab
  2. Tap on the Settings icon
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision

Processing Time

Your data deletion request will be processed within 30 days. You will be notified via email when the process is complete.

For Facebook Login Users

If you signed in with your Facebook account, you can also revoke data access through Facebook:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings & Privacy
  2. Select Settings > Apps and Websites
  3. Find the Reso application and click "Remove"
