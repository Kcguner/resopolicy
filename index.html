Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

This privacy policy explains what information the Reso mobile application ("App") collects from users and how this information is used.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • First and last name
  • Social media profile information (when signing in with Google or Facebook)

Device Information

  • Location: Your GPS location to show nearby venues
  • Camera: To add venue photos (optional)
  • Microphone: To measure venue noise levels (optional)

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show venues near you
  • To personalize your app experience
  • To manage user-submitted venue content

3. Advertising

Our app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers to personalize the advertising experience. For more information, please review the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Premium Subscription

The app offers an optional premium subscription. Payment processing is handled through Google Play Store or Apple App Store, and your financial information is not stored by us.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored on secure servers.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (database and authentication)
  • Mapbox (map services)
  • Google AdMob (advertisements)
  • Google Sign-In (login option)
  • Facebook Login (login option)

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from this age group.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Close your account

9. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes.
